About Me

Based in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Violet Patterson is a professional model and actress with over 5 years of experience in the fashion, commercial, and entertainment industrises. Known for her professionalism and versatility, Violet has worked on a wide range of modeling assignments, from high-fashion editorial shoots to runway shows and promotional campaigns. She specializes in showcasing a variety of garments, including evening gowns, lingerie, swimwear, and hosiery, with a particular focus on bridal wear and intimate apparel.

In addition to her modeling expertise, Violet is an accomplished dancer with a strong foundation in ballet and jazz. She has performed in several successful ballet productions, demonstrating grace, precision, and stage presence. Her dance background enhances her ability to convey movement, elegance, and emotion in modeling and acting projects, setting her apart as a multifaceted talent in the industry.

Violet's dedication to excellence and her ability to adapt to diverse creative demands make her a sought-after professional. She is committed to delivering high-quality work and is always eager to embrace new opportunities that allow her to expand her artistic range and professional growth.
