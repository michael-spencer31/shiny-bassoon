Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you based?

I am based in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada. However, I am available for modeling and dance assignments across Canada.

What type of modeling assignments do you accept?

I specialize in a variety of modeling work, including:

• Bridal Wear (Gowns, Lingerie, and Shoes)

• Evening gowns and high-fashion editorial pieces

• Lingerie and intimates

• Swimwear

• Hosiery (Pantyhose, Stockings, Tights)

I also accept assignments involving sheer or transparent tops or going topless, depending on the project. I do not, under any circumstances, accept fully nude or bottomless assignments.

Do you provide your own wardrobe?

I can usually provide basic items like hosiery or a simple bra and panty set for assignments. However, most garments are typically provided by the client or designer. Specific wardrobe needs can be discussed and agreed upon before the assignment to ensure all requirements are met.

What are your clothing sizes?

My clothing sizes can be foudn on the Home Page. For bridal or formal gowns, precise measurements are often required. These can be provided in advance or taken at your event. For optimal accuracy, I recommend having measurements taken by an experienced seamstress.
