Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions
Where is Violet located? Does she travel for assignments? +

Violet is based in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada. However, she is available for modeling and dance assignments across Canada. Please note due to Visa issues Violet is unable to do assignments outside of Canada.

What type of modeling work does Violet specialise in? +
  • Bridal Wear (Gowns, Lingerie, and Shoes)
  • Evening Gowns and High-Fashion editorial pieces
  • Lingerie and Intimates
  • Swimwear
  • Hosiery (Pantyhose, Stockings, Tights)
Does Violet provide her own wardrobe? +
No. If a simple item like a bra/panty for under a dress or hosiery is needed Violet can usually provide it, but it is expected the client will provide all garments necessary for the assignment.

For footwear, Violet will often bring her own to ensure optimal fit and comfort. Violet owns an extensive collection of footwear including heels, sandals, boots, etc. but this should be discussed beforehand.
What are Violet's clothing sizes? +
Most of Violet's clothing sizes can be found on the Home page. Bridal and Evening Gowns usually require exact measurements. For best results, these should be taken by an experienced seamstress prior to the event date.
Booking and Rates
How can Violet be booked for an event or photoshoot? +
Clients can contact Violet through the form on her website or email her directly with details of the event, including location, duration, and wardrobe requirements.
What details should be provided when contacting Violet for a project? +
When contacting Violet, clients should provide the event type, location, duration, wardrobe requirements, and whether professional hair and makeup services will be available. This information helps her prepare effectively and provide an accurate estimate.
What are Violet's rates, and how are they calculated? +
Violet's general rates start at $100/hour CAD. Rates may vary based on travel distance, event duration, wardrobe needs, and the type of attire required.

Violet or a member of her team calculate the cost for each event on a case-by-case basis.

The Rates page can be used to get an estimate of the cost for your event.
Are travel and accommodation expenses included in Violet's rates? +
No. Violet does not charge travel or accommodation expenses for events located within 2 hours of Halifax, Nova Scotia, but expenses will be added to the final cost for other events.
Custom Requests
Can Violet model multiple styles (e.g., bridal and casual) during one event? +
Yes, Violet can model multiple styles during an event if discussed and planned beforehand. However, additional time may be required to accommodate wardrobe changes and adjustments to hair and makeup.
Are there any types of modeling assignments Violet does not accept? +
Violet is generally happy to accept any type of modeling assignments, ranging from anything from evening gowns to lingerie. Violet is also willing to model sheer or transparent lingerie.

Please note that Violet reserves the right to not accept any assignment that does not meet her personal or artisitc standards.
