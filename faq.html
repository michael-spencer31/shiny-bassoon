Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located? Do you travel for assignments? +

Violet is based in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada. However, she is available for modeling and dance assignments across Canada. Please note due to Visa issues Violet is unable to do assignments outside of Canada.

What type of modeling assignments do you accept? +

Violet specializes in a variety of modeling work, including:

  • Bridal Wear (Gowns, Lingerie, and Shoes)
  • Evening Gowns and High-Fashion editorial pieces
  • Lingerie and Intimates
  • Swimwear
  • Hosiery (Pantyhose, Stockings, Tights)

This list is not exhaustive; if you have questions about a specific type of attire, please reach out!

Do you provide your own wardrobe? +

I can usually provide basic items like hosiery or a simple bra and panty set for assignments. However, most garments are typically provided by the client or designer. Specific wardrobe needs can be discussed and agreed upon before the assignment to ensure all requirements are met.

What are you clothing sizes? +

Most of my clothing sizes can be found on the Home page. Bridal and Evening Gowns usually require exact measurements. For best results, these should be taken by an experienced seamstress prior to the event date.
